Egemen TAS

CEO & Co-Founder

Leadership

Egemen Tas is the co-founder and CEO of Netzilo, a pioneering cybersecurity startup focused on delivering innovative network security solutions. Prior to founding Netzilo, Egemen was a Managing Director of Risk & Financial Advisory at Deloitte, where he joined through the acquisition of his zero trust network access company TransientX, Inc. At Netzilo, Egemen leads the company to creating secure and scalable solutions that empower businesses to embrace generative AI and SaaS. Drawing on his extensive experience as the Founder & CEO of TransientX and Senior Vice President of Engineering at Comodo, Egemen's leadership is marked by a commitment to excellence and a vision for a more secure digital future.
